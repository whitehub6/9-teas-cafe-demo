9 TEA'S

Chai,Conversations & Bites

Established in 2019, 9 Tea's Cafe is Bhayandar's favorite youth hangout. We blend the warmth of freshly brewed traditional Indian teas and rich cold coffees with a delicious menu of fast-food favorites—from cheese-loaded pizzas and grilled sandwiches to sizzling hot snacks. It's more than a cafe; it's a cozy space where friends meet, ideas brew, and moments turn into memories.
